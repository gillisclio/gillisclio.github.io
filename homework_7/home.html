What's More...

More bees, more money, more awareness

Honeybees polinate 70 of the around 100 crop species that feed 90% of the world. Just wow.

In fact, the presence and absence of honeybees tells us when there has been a shift in the ecological balance, which impacts food production.

The economic health of U.S. agriculture depends on this balance – honey bees are responsible for $30 billion a year in crops.

(Not to mention that almonds literally would not exist without honeybees to pollinate almond trees.)

Unfortunately, the future of the honeybee is at risk.
